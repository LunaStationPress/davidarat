David, a Rat

by Elizabeth Hinckley

David is a very sick boy. As his illness starts to change him by taking away his ability to enjoy the life he once loved, his beloved Grandpa steps in with a very special gift meant to help him find a reason to live again. The only thing is, Grandpa died last year. And David is the only one who can see him.

About Elizabeth

Elizabeth Hinckley is a teller of stories, hiding in plain sight as a neighbor, co-worker, student, and secret adventurer. She has spent several years researching the areas between NY’s Hudson Valley and Philadelphia, and many more studying how to listen with love to the secrets of things living, and those who used to be. She lives in NJ with her family, including beloved animal members, of course. This is her first novel.
